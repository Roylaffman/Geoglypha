About Graphitia

Graphitia is a dedicated gallery for black and white artwork that explores unique visual expressions. Our collection showcases various styles and techniques, emphasizing contrast, form, and composition without the distraction of color.

This gallery is part of the broader Geoglypha project, which explores the intersection of history, archaeology, and geography through various visual mediums.
